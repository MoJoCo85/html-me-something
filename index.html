My Journey From Georgia to Missouri

I was born and raised in Thomasville, Georgia on October 3, 1985. There, I grew up playing multiple sports and quickly develeped a passion for athletics. I mainly ran track, wrestled, and played football.

   

My success in athletics landed me in Williamsburg, Kentucky at The University of the Cumberlands. There I continued to excel in sports, earned my B.S. in Exercise Science, and eventually met my wife. Life after college happend and my wife and I bore our first born child, Micah. Tragedy struck, and we moved back to Georgia for a short time before my wife decided to finish her degree.

 

My wife's old coach from The University of the Cumberlands was now the head coach at Lindenwood University in Missouri so she decided to go there. I followed shortly after in October of 2011. We settled down, found a church, grew our roots and we've been here every since!
